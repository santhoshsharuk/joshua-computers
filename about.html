Our Story

Joshua Computers was founded with a simple mission: to make premium laptops accessible to everyone. We believe that quality technology shouldn't break the bank.

Over the years, we've helped hundreds of students, professionals, and businesses find their perfect laptop match. Our expertise in both new and refurbished devices ensures you get the best value for your money.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted laptop retailers, known for our quality products, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service.
